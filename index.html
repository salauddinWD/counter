Death rate is very high due to lack of blood

Bag of blood is required on 13th every year. About 55,000 people died without receiving blood. 35-40% of normal blood is obtained from patient's relatives, 15-20% from volunteers and 15-20% from blood vendors. The remaining 20-25 percent or about 2.5 weeks of bags of blood remain in short supply. Only 15-20 percent of blood supplied to healthy vendors is unsafe. The result is about 40 percent or about 5 days of deficient and unsafe blood votes.With this app we are trying to fill the gap as much as possible.

Blood donation rate

According to the World Health Organization, 45% of people in the developed world voluntarily donate blood. And in Bangladesh or developing countries, only three people donate blood per thousand. In Bangladesh, only 5 lakh bags need to be collected annually to meet the shortage and stop the use of unsafe blood. A healthy person can donate blood three times a year. According to that, only 2 lakh people out of 16 crore people can be motivated as voluntary blood donors to solve this problem. And this app is created to solve this problem
